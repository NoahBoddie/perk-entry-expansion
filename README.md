Entry points so far
SaveScrollChance- Chance to not consume a scroll
BarterStolenItems- The ability to barter with specific items that are stolen
BarterUnrelatedItems- The ability to barter with specific items that are unrelated to the keywords of the vendor
AddMerchantWares- The ability to distribute items to a vendor when their stock resets
ModBuyItemPrice/ModSellItemPrice- The ability to adjust the base price of a specific item
AddContainerItem- The ability to add items to a container
Multishot- The ability to shoot more than one arrow at once. Currently bugged and has a freeze associated with it's use
MagicApplyCombatSpell- Apply a spell when a casted spell hits someone
PotionApplySpell- Apply a spell when a user consumes a potion
